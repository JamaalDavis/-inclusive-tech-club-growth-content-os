# Company — Inclusive Tech Club

## What We Do

Inclusive Tech Club helps product teams, designers, and business leaders build products and systems that work for everyone — not just the default user.

We do this through:
- Content (LinkedIn, newsletter, frameworks, toolkits)
- Workshops and training
- Consulting and advisory
- Community

## Stage

Early-stage. Growing. Bootstrapped. Operated by Jamaal Davis.

The content engine is the primary growth lever right now. The goal is to build an audience of practitioners and decision-makers who trust Inclusive Tech Club as a source of rigorous, practical, business-relevant inclusive design thinking.

## Core Competitive Position

Most content about accessibility and inclusive design:
- Treats it as a compliance topic
- Focuses only on visual and technical accessibility
- Ignores business stakes
- Ignores power dynamics
- Is written for other accessibility specialists, not for product teams and leaders

Inclusive Tech Club is different because:
- We connect inclusion to revenue, risk, and operational reality
- We treat accessibility as one part of a larger design justice commitment
- We write for people who need to make decisions and win arguments, not just feel inspired
- We are willing to name uncomfortable systemic realities
