# Offer Map

## Current Offers

### Lead Magnet: Inclusive Design Value Map (Planned)
- **Format:** PDF / interactive framework
- **Value:** Helps product teams connect specific design decisions to business outcomes
- **Audience:** Product leaders, design leads
- **CTA target:** Email capture → nurture sequence → workshop inquiry

### Newsletter: Inclusive Tech Club Weekly (Active)
- **Format:** Email newsletter
- **Value:** Weekly dose of practical inclusive design, AI critique, and power dynamics in product work
- **Audience:** Practitioners and leaders
- **CTA target:** LinkedIn → newsletter signup

## Planned Offers (Validation Phase)

### Workshop: Inclusive Design Value Case
- **Format:** Half-day workshop (virtual or in-person)
- **Audience:** Product teams, design teams, leadership
- **Problem it solves:** Teams know inclusion matters but cannot make the internal business case
- **Content that validates this offer:** Pillar 1 posts, Value Map lead magnet engagement

### Workshop: Human-Centered AI Prototyping
- **Format:** Half-day to full-day workshop
- **Audience:** Product and AI teams
- **Problem it solves:** Teams building AI products without structured thinking about harm, bias, and oversight
- **Content that validates this offer:** Pillar 2 posts, AI governance content engagement

### Consulting: Inclusive Design Strategy
- **Format:** Ongoing advisory or project-based engagement
- **Audience:** Startups and scale-ups
- **Problem it solves:** No internal inclusive design expertise; need external credibility and strategy

### Course: Inclusive Design for Product Teams (Future)
- **Format:** Self-paced online course
- **Audience:** Product managers and designers who want structured learning
- **Validation:** Newsletter subscriber interest, DM signals, poll data

### Community: Inclusive Tech Club (Future)
- **Format:** Paid community (Discord or Circle)
- **Audience:** Practitioners who want peer connection and ongoing resources
- **Validation:** Newsletter engagement, LinkedIn comments
