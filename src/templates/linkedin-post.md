## Format Notes

**Short post (300–600 characters)**
- One idea
- No CTA needed
- Strong hook + 2–3 sentence insight + optional question

**Medium post (600–1800 characters)**
- Full arc: hook → problem → insight → lesson → CTA
- Max 4 short paragraphs
- Use line breaks aggressively

**Carousel intro post**
- Intro copy only — the carousel does the work
- Hook + tease of first insight + "Swipe for..." CTA
- Keep it tight

## Voice Checklist

Before submitting for review, confirm:

- [ ] No banned phrases (run voice checker)
- [ ] Opening does not start with "I want to talk about" or "Today I..."
- [ ] No vague statistics without citation
- [ ] Includes systems-level thinking (who benefits, who pays, what maintains this)
- [ ] Sounds like Jamaal, not a SaaS homepage
