## HOOK (5–10 seconds)

**Narration:** [Start mid-thought. No "Hey everyone." Drop into the uncomfortable truth or cost. One sentence.]

**On-Screen Text:** [Key claim in 5–7 words. High contrast against background.]

**Scene Direction:** [Talking head / B-roll / text animation. Face visible for lip-reading accessibility.]

**Accessibility Notes:** Captions from first frame. No flashing content.

---

## BODY SECTION 1 — THE PROBLEM (~12–15 seconds)

**Narration:** [Name the specific problem. Plain language. 2–3 short sentences.]

**On-Screen Text:** [Name of the problem or the cost. 5–7 words.]

**Scene Direction:** [Talking head or text animation. Keep background uncluttered.]

**Accessibility Notes:** Captions synchronized with narration. On-screen text must not conflict with captions placement.

---

## BODY SECTION 2 — THE INSIGHT (~12–15 seconds)

**Narration:** [The reframe. State it directly. Give the viewer a beat to process.]

**On-Screen Text:** [The insight in 6–8 words.]

**Scene Direction:** [Consider pausing movement here to let the insight land.]

**Accessibility Notes:** Pause long enough for caption readers to keep up. Minimum caption display time: 1.5 seconds per sentence.

---

## BODY SECTION 3 — THE TAKEAWAY (~10–12 seconds)

**Narration:** [The one thing they will remember. "The question to ask is..." or "The rule of thumb here is..."]

**On-Screen Text:** [The takeaway sentence in full.]

**Scene Direction:** [Slow down. Let this breathe.]

**Accessibility Notes:** Full sentence on screen, not truncated. Caption timing matches narration.

---

## CTA (5–12 seconds)

**Narration:** [One clear ask. What they get. Why now.]

**On-Screen Text:** [CTA text + handle or link. High contrast.]

**Scene Direction:** [Text card or lower-third overlay. Link visible on screen and spoken aloud.]

**Accessibility Notes:** CTA must be spoken AND shown on screen. Link text must be descriptive.

## Caption Guidance

- Auto-captions require review and correction before publishing
- Caption font: minimum 22px equivalent, white text / black outline
- Caption accuracy target: 99%+
- Speaker identified if multiple speakers
- Non-speech sounds described: [applause], [music]
- Caption format for LinkedIn: SRT preferred

## Video Accessibility Checklist

- [ ] Hook does not start with "Hey everyone" or "Today I want to..."
- [ ] Captions reviewed and corrected (not auto-caption quality)
- [ ] On-screen text meets 4.5:1 contrast
- [ ] No flashing content (max 3 flashes/second)
- [ ] Face visible for lip-reading during narration
- [ ] Background audio/music below 20% volume
- [ ] CTA spoken aloud AND displayed on screen
- [ ] Video does not rely on color alone to convey information
