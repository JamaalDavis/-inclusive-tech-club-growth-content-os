# Nurture Email Template

Use this template for CRM nurture sequence emails for Inclusive Tech Club.

---

## Subject Line

[Specific and useful. No clickbait. The subscriber should know what they are opening before they open it. Max 50 characters for mobile preview.]

## Preview Text

[One sentence. Extends the subject line. Never duplicates it. Max 90 characters.]

---

## Opening

[1–3 sentences. Warm, not corporate. Context for why this email now. Does not start with "I hope this email finds you well." Does not start with the subscriber's name as the first word.]

---

## Core Content

[The value. 2–4 paragraphs. One idea. Practical, not theoretical.

This is the reason they subscribed. Do not waste it on logistics or event announcements unless that is the explicit purpose of this email.]

---

## Inclusive Design Angle

[Connect the content to inclusive design, accessibility, or business equity. Even in a nurture email, there should be a real insight — not just a warm touch.]

---

## Action Step

[One ask. Not three. Reply to this email, read this article, download this resource, book this call.

Be specific about what they get by doing it.]

---

## Closing

[1–2 sentences. Human. Sounds like Jamaal. Not "Best regards" or "Kind regards." Not "Talk soon!" as a cover for saying nothing.]

## Email Accessibility Checklist

- [ ] Subject line and preview text are descriptive and non-deceptive
- [ ] HTML email uses semantic structure (heading tags, not just styled text)
- [ ] All images have alt text
- [ ] Links are descriptive, not "click here" or "read more"
- [ ] Plain text version available
- [ ] Minimum font size 14px
- [ ] Sufficient contrast for body text and headings
- [ ] Unsubscribe link present and functional
- [ ] CTA button has descriptive text and adequate touch target (44x44px minimum)
