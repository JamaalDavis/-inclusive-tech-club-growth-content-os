# Carousel Template

Use this template for Inclusive Tech Club LinkedIn carousel outlines.

---

## Slide 1 — HOOK

**Headline:** [Strong opening statement. The uncomfortable truth or surprising cost. Short. No windup.]

**Body:** [1–2 sentences that earn the swipe. Name the specific problem. Do not summarize what you're about to cover.]

**Visual Direction:** Bold text on clean background. High contrast (min 4.5:1). No decorative images competing with headline.

**Alt Text:** [Full description of visual content + complete text on screen. Read by screen readers.]

---

## Slide 2 — THE PROBLEM

**Headline:** [Name the problem clearly. A statement, not a question.]

**Body:** [2–3 sentences. Specific. Who experiences this? What does it cost?]

**Visual Direction:** Text-primary. Supporting icon or minimal illustration. No stock photos.

**Alt Text:** [Full text + visual description]

---

## Slide 3 — WHY IT HAPPENS / ROOT CAUSE

**Headline:** [Name the structural cause — not user error, system design.]

**Body:** [2–3 sentences. What incentives or defaults maintain this problem?]

**Visual Direction:** Clean text layout. One cause, named clearly.

**Alt Text:** [Full text + visual description]

---

## Slide 4 — THE INSIGHT

**Headline:** [The reframe. The thing most teams get wrong.]

**Body:** [2–3 sentences. Practical. Something they can use.]

**Visual Direction:** Pull quote treatment or contrast-heavy typographic layout. Alt text must contain full quote.

**Alt Text:** [Full text + visual description]

---

## Slide 5 — THE FRAMEWORK

**Headline:** [Name the framework in 5 words or fewer.]

**Body:** [3–5 bullet points or numbered steps. Plain language. Each item standalone-readable.]

**Visual Direction:** Simple numbered or bulleted list. Strong heading hierarchy. List items described in alt text.

**Alt Text:** [Full list contents + visual description]

---

## Slide 6 — PRACTICAL APPLICATION

**Headline:** [What to do. Starts with a verb.]

**Body:** [2–3 specific, actionable steps. Not "think about" — "add to your brief", "ask this question", "run this audit".]

**Visual Direction:** Checklist or step layout. Visual hierarchy matches logical order.

**Alt Text:** [Full checklist content + visual description]

---

## Slide 7 — CTA

**Headline:** [One clear ask. Download, subscribe, follow, or book.]

**Body:** [1 sentence. Specific benefit of taking the action.]

**Visual Direction:** High-contrast CTA. Action text meets 3:1 contrast. Alt text contains full CTA.

**Alt Text:** [Full CTA text + visual description]

## Carousel Accessibility Checklist

- [ ] All slides have complete alt text
- [ ] All text meets 4.5:1 contrast ratio (3:1 for large text)
- [ ] Minimum font size equivalent to 16px
- [ ] No text embedded in images without text alternative
- [ ] CTA is readable without relying on color alone
- [ ] Information hierarchy matches logical reading order
- [ ] Visual design does not require color perception to understand
